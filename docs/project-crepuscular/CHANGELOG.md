## 2026-07-15 — Foundation locked

### Added

- Project name: **Project Crepuscular**
- North Star
- Brand Soul
- Four Pillars
- Product and design principles
- Brand personality
- Voice rules
- Initial Visual Language chapter
- Decision log

### Locked decisions

- Vesper is not designed to maximize time in-app.
- Closing the app ready to observe is a successful outcome.
- The intro may dazzle; daily use should soothe.
- Scientific information comes first; poetry is limited to a closing line.
- Gold is earned and cannot become a default decorative accent.
- Vesper never guesses, begs, repeats itself, or spends attention without paying it back.
- The design-system chapter remains named **Visual Language**.

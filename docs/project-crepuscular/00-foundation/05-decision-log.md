# Decision Log

This file records durable decisions so they are not repeatedly debated without new evidence.

## 2026-07-15 — Design-system name

**Decision:** Use **Project Crepuscular**.

**Reason:** It directly connects Vesper to twilight, the evening star, the intro, and the brand palette. “Interstellar Terminus” was more fictional and less specific to Vesper.

**Status:** Approved.

---

## 2026-07-15 — Document structure

**Decision:** Use many focused Markdown files, one root `README.md`, and one `CHANGELOG.md`.

**Reason:** The system must remain searchable, maintainable, versionable, and easy to hand to future designers, developers, or AI collaborators.

**Status:** Approved.

---

## 2026-07-15 — North Star

**Decision:** Vesper does not optimize primarily for time in app. It prepares users to return to the real sky.

**Reason:** The product succeeds when people close it ready to observe and share what they learned.

**Status:** Approved.

---

## 2026-07-15 — Gold usage

**Decision:** Gold is earned.

**Reason:** If gold becomes common, it loses emotional and semantic value.

**Status:** Approved.

---

## 2026-07-15 — Experience rhythm

**Decision:** The intro may dazzle; daily use should soothe.

**Reason:** First launch establishes wonder. Repeated use must become calm, fast, and trustworthy.

**Status:** Approved.

---

## 2026-07-15 — Copy order

**Decision:** Scientific information first; at most one poetic closing line.

**Reason:** Vesper must remain useful, retellable, and credible.

**Status:** Approved.

---

## 2026-07-15 — Chapter name

**Decision:** Keep the chapter name **Visual Language**.

**Reason:** It is clear, conventional, and immediately understandable.

**Status:** Approved.

# Paywall and Pro

**Status:** Draft for review  
**Version:** 0.1

## Purpose

The paywall explains Pro without weakening the promise that the sky remains free.

## Core Principle

> Everything needed to look up is free. Pro helps people plan, shoot, and chase.

## Entry

The paywall appears only after:

- the user taps a Pro feature
- the user chooses to view Pro
- a clear free limit is reached

It does not appear:

- during onboarding
- before the user sees Tonight
- during eclipse safety
- as an interstitial
- after routine success
- through a fake countdown

## Message

Primary English direction:

> See further.

Supporting statement:

> The sky is free — and stays free. Pro is for those who chase it.

The free promise must be visible on the paywall.

## Content Order

1. value statement
2. selected Pro capability
3. concise Pro benefits
4. what remains free forever
5. pricing
6. purchase action
7. restore purchases
8. terms and privacy

## Pro Benefits

May include:

- aurora early warning
- live solar-wind detail
- astro-weather
- seven-night planning
- equipment-matched photography targets
- unlimited locations
- advanced widgets
- full satellite catalog
- observation export and statistics

Do not list dozens of minor features.

## Pricing

Current direction:

- Yearly
- Lifetime
- no weekly
- no monthly
- no credits
- no ads

Regional pricing should remain fair.

## Trial

A trial may appear only after a Pro-feature tap.

Rules:

- conversion date is clear
- price after trial is clear
- no silent ambiguity
- cancellation path is standard
- no fake urgency

## Founder Offer

If used:

- exact limit is factual
- terms are visible
- no resettable countdown
- no misleading scarcity

## Purchase States

Define:

- loading products
- products unavailable
- purchasing
- success
- cancelled
- failed
- restore success
- restore unavailable
- already subscribed

Purchase success should return the user to the feature they wanted.

Do not trap them on a celebration screen.

## Visual Treatment

The paywall may be more polished than routine settings, but remains Daily Instrument Mode.

Allowed:

- one atmospheric hero
- one earned gold action
- restrained comparison
- calm motion

Not allowed:

- casino-like pricing cards
- flashing discount
- fake crossed-out prices
- rotating testimonials
- countdown pressure
- several glowing CTAs

## Accessibility

- prices are announced with billing period
- yearly and lifetime choices are explicit
- free features are readable
- legal links are reachable
- purchase state is announced
- color is not the only selected-plan indicator

## Anti-Patterns

Never:

- paywall onboarding
- paywall eclipse safety
- hide the free list
- use weekly pricing
- use fake scarcity
- make restore purchases difficult
- move promised free features behind Pro
- interrupt observation with an upgrade modal

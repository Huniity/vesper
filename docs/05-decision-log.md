# Brand

**Status:** Approved  
**Version:** 1.0

## Name

**Vesper**

Vesper refers to the evening star and the arrival of evening. The identity belongs to twilight: the moment the ordinary day opens into the observable sky.

## Design-System Name

**Project Crepuscular**

Crepuscular means relating to twilight. The name connects the system directly to Vesper's core moment, palette, atmosphere, and emotional transition.

## Brand Idea

> The evening is not the end of the day. It is the beginning of observation.

## Personality

Vesper combines:

- the wayfinding knowledge of an old celestial navigator
- the perspective of an astronaut
- the restraint of a careful scientific instrument
- the warmth of a patient guide

## Brand Attributes

- calm
- precise
- cinematic
- honest
- spatial
- scientific
- quietly premium
- approachable
- timeless

## Vesper Is

- a rooftop at dusk
- a lighthouse that keeps watch
- a window into the real sky
- a guide that prepares people to observe
- a daily ritual of curiosity

## Vesper Is Not

- cyberpunk
- a space game
- a fictional spacecraft console
- a generic technology dashboard
- an attention trap
- a planetarium replacement
- a neon science-fiction interface
- a content feed designed for endless scrolling

## Signature Ideas

- the evening horizon
- the evening star
- earned gold
- quiet darkness
- celestial geometry
- measured information
- pointing rather than lecturing
